Frequently Asked Questions

ElectricalFlo is operated by ElectricalFlo LLC.

Will my radio fit in the cup holder you're selling?

We list compatible batteries for each cup holder whenever possible. If your setup isn’t listed, we can customize a cup holder to fit your exact radio and battery.

Will the cup holder fit inside my vehicle’s cup holder?

Our designs are made to fit standard vehicle cup holders, typically between 78–81mm in diameter. If you have a unique setup, we can customize the fit to match your vehicle.

Can I add my callsign?

Yes. All cup holders support custom callsigns, including glow-in-the-dark options in green, blue, and red/orange. Just include your callsign and color choice in the notes at checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Processing time depends on order volume, but most orders ship within 24–48 hours. Custom orders may take up to 7 days depending on complexity.

Do you support radios not listed?

Yes. Select the “Custom Radio Cup Holder (Made to Order)” option and include your radio model, battery model, desired callsign, and vehicle details in the notes at checkout. We’ll follow up by email to make sure everything is built exactly to your needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We use Stripe for secure checkout, which supports all major credit cards and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer refunds?

We strive to accommodate every customer as much as possible; however, all sales are final. If you experience any issues with your order, please contact us and we will work with you to make it right, including providing a replacement if necessary.
