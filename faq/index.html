Frequently Asked Questions

Wil my radio fit in the cup holder you're selling?

Mostly Yes. I try and include the compatible batteries that will work with the cup holders but if your battery is not listed, we can always customize the cup holder to your exact needs.

Can I add my callsign?

Yes. Most products support custom callsigns including glow-in-the-dark options.

How long does shipping take?

Processing time varies depending on order volume, but I aim to ship as quickly as possible. Typical leads times are 24-48hours after order is received. Custom orders can take up to 7 days depending on complexity.

Do you support radios not listed?

Yes. There is an item named, "Custom Radio Cup Holder (Made to Order)". Place an order with that item and in the notes section at checkout, include all of your details. Radio Model, Battery Model, What callsign you'd like, What year and model vehicle the cup holder will be going in. I will follow up via email to get your cup holder dialed in to your exact needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

Stripe (card), PayPal, and Venmo are supported.
